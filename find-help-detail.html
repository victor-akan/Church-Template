You Don't Have to Keep Carrying This Alone

Whatever you're feeling right now — the pain, the confusion, the heaviness — it's real. It's valid. And it doesn't have to stay this way forever. This message was chosen because people in exactly your situation have found hope in it.

Watch it when you have a quiet moment. You don't have to prepare. You don't have to believe anything yet. Just listen — and let the words do what they need to do.
