Where Your Gift Goes

Every Dollar Has a Purpose

We believe in full financial transparency. Here's how contributions are used to serve our community and extend God's kingdom.

⛪
Ministry & Programs — 55%
Preaching, worship, children's ministry, youth, outreach programs, and pastoral care.
🏠
Facilities & Operations — 25%
Maintaining and improving the spaces where community happens.
🌍
Community Outreach — 15%
Food programs, family support, and local partnerships with other nonprofits.
🙏
Benevolence — 5%
Helping members and community members in urgent financial need.
