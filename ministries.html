Where Do You Belong?

From children to seniors, there is a place for every person at Grace Community. Find your community, discover your purpose, and grow together.

Ministry is not a program — it's a family. Our ministries exist to help you find a circle where you are truly known, equipped for your calling, and connected to something bigger than yourself.

Your First Step

How to Get Plugged In

Finding your place shouldn't be complicated. Here's how we help every new person discover where they truly belong.

1
Visit on a Sunday

Come to a service and experience the community. No pressure — just come and see.

2
Attend Newcomers' Connect

Our monthly newcomers' gathering is designed to help you find your fit. Meet the team, explore the ministries, ask questions.

3
Join a Home Group

Small groups are where real community happens. We'll match you with a group based on your neighbourhood, life stage, and interests.

4
Discover Your Gifts

Take our free Gifts & Calling assessment online and meet with a team leader to find where you can serve.

🙋

Ready to Serve?

We have volunteer opportunities in every area — from welcoming first-time visitors to helping coordinate events to serving food in the community.
