You're Welcome Here

Plan Your First Visit

We know walking into a new church can feel like a big step. We want to make it as simple, warm, and pressure-free as possible. Here's everything you need to know.

I'm Planning to Come

Let us know you're coming and we'll make sure someone is ready to welcome you personally.

No spam. We'll send you one friendly welcome email and be ready for you on Sunday.

No Surprises

What to Expect

👋
A Warm Welcome

From the parking lot to the front door, our Welcome Team is there. If you let us know you're coming, we'll have someone specifically looking out for you.

👔
Come as You Are

Seriously — there is no dress code. Some people come in suits. Some come in jeans. We just want you comfortable and present.

🎶
Worship & Music

Contemporary worship music — energetic, heartfelt, and led by a talented team. You can sing along, stand quietly, or just take it in. All of that is welcome.

📖
Real, Practical Teaching

Our messages are honest, biblically grounded, and directly applicable to real life. No jargon. No shouting. Just truth told with love.

🧒
Children Are Cared For

Kids from birth through grade 6 have their own age-appropriate ministry during the service. Safe, fun, and full of joy. You'll need to register them at the Kids Desk on arrival.

⏱
How Long Is the Service?

Services typically run 75–90 minutes. Worship: ~25 minutes. Announcements: ~5 minutes. Message: ~40 minutes. Response & prayer: ~10 minutes.

🅿️
Parking

Free parking is available in our main lot and the overflow lot on Grace Lane. Ushers are available to help on busy Sundays. Arrive 10–15 minutes early to settle in.

A Typical Sunday

Your Morning, Step by Step

🅿️
Arrive Early
Park & Be Welcomed

Pull in and our parking team will guide you. Greeters at the entrance will say hello and point you in the right direction.

☕
Pre-Service
Coffee & Connections

Grab a free coffee or tea in the foyer. It's a relaxed, friendly environment — no pressure to perform or be anything other than yourself.

🎶
Service Begins
Worship

The service opens with 4–5 songs. Stand, sit, sing along — however you feel comfortable engaging.

📖
Mid-Service
The Message

Our pastor delivers a practical, scripture-based message. We encourage you to bring a Bible or use our app to follow along.

🙏
Closing
Response & Prayer

We close with a time of response — an opportunity for personal prayer, communion (monthly), and connection. No pressure. Just space.

👥
After Service
First-Time Guest Welcome

We'd love to meet you! Head to the First Time Guest desk in the foyer for a welcome pack, introductions to our team, and answers to any questions.

Quick Answers

Do I need to book a seat in advance?

No booking required — walk-ins are always welcome. If you fill out our visit form, we'll just make sure someone is looking out for you.

What about my children?

Children ages 0–12 have their own ministry during the service. You'll check them in at the Kids Desk when you arrive. It's a safe, vetted environment.

Will I be asked to give money?

We never pressure first-time guests to give. An offering is collected during the service — participation is entirely optional and never required.

Is it okay if I'm not a Christian?

Absolutely. We welcome people at every point on their spiritual journey — curious, skeptical, or exploring for the first time. Come as you are.
